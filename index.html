Morphology Match

How to Play

Morphology Match is a word puzzle game where you must match words with shared morphological features across multiple rows.

  1. Shuffle the word rows using the "Shuffle Rows" button.
  2. Click the "Check Matches" button to identify matching words.
  3. Your score is cumulative across all rounds.
  4. Advance to the next round by clicking the "Next Round" button.
  5. Click the "Start Over" button to reset the game to the first round and clear your score.
